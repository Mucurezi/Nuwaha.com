About Our School

Our History

Muteesa II Memorial School - Mutundwe was established to provide quality education to the community. Named after the late Kabaka Muteesa II, our school upholds values of excellence, integrity, and service.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and leadership skills in every student.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals ready to contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Integrity and honesty
  • Respect and discipline
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Community service
